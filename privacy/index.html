2. What data we collect

Three sources, in plain terms:

  • Free deliverability audit form ({{ROOT}}free-deliverability-audit/): your email address, the domain you want audited, and your name (optional).
  • Contact & quote form: whatever you submit, typically email, project description, and any details you choose to include.

No analytics, no marketing pixels, no third-party trackers, no behavioral profiling. The site does not set first-party tracking cookies.

Legal basis under GDPR (for EU residents): legitimate interest for delivering requested content; explicit consent for any further marketing communications; contractual necessity for service delivery.
